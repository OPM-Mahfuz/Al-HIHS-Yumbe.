Community Health Program

Program Description

The Community Health program focuses on training students to address health issues at the community level through outreach, health education, disease prevention, and health promotion activities. Graduates will play vital roles in improving health awareness and access to services in underserved and refugee communities.

Curriculum Overview

  • Introduction to Community Health
  • Health Education and Promotion
  • Epidemiology and Disease Prevention
  • Maternal and Child Health
  • Environmental Health
  • Fieldwork and Community Engagement
  • Research Methods in Public Health

Entry Requirements

  • Ugandan Certificate of Education (UCE) with passes in English, Biology, Chemistry, and Mathematics
  • Minimum of 5 passes in O-Level
  • Relevant health-related diploma or certificate (preferred)
  • Age: 17 years and above
  • Interview and health screening
