Program Overview

The Certificate in Laboratory Technology program trains students in laboratory procedures, diagnostic techniques, and safety protocols essential for supporting medical diagnostics and research.

Curriculum

  • Introduction to Laboratory Science
  • Laboratory Equipment and Safety
  • Sample Collection and Analysis
  • Diagnostic Techniques
  • Practical Training & Internships

Admission Requirements

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Minimum GPA requirement
  • Application fee
  • Interview process

Duration & Fees

Duration: 1 year
Fees: USD 2,000 (subject to change)
