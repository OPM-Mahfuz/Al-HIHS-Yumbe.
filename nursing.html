Nursing Program

Program Description

The Nursing program at Al-Hassan Institute of Health Science aims to equip students with the essential skills and knowledge required to provide comprehensive nursing care in various healthcare settings. The program emphasizes practical skills, ethical standards, and community health principles to prepare competent and compassionate nurses who can serve in both urban and rural environments.

Curriculum Overview

  • Foundation in Anatomy, Physiology, and Microbiology
  • Nursing Fundamentals and Patient Care
  • Pharmacology and Medication Administration
  • Maternal and Child Health Nursing
  • Community and Public Health Nursing
  • Medical-Surgical Nursing
  • Clinical Practice and Internship
  • Ethics and Professional Practice

Entry Requirements

  • Ugandan Certificate of Education (UCE) with passes in English, Mathematics, Biology, and Chemistry
  • Minimum of 5 passes in O-Level (including English, Mathematics, Biology, Chemistry, and Physics)
  • Completion of a relevant pre-nursing certificate or diploma (for advanced entry)
  • Age: 17 years and above
  • Pass an interview and health screening
