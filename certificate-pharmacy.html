Program Overview

The Certificate in Pharmacy program equips students with knowledge of medication dispensing, pharmaceutical practices, and patient counseling to support healthcare teams effectively.

Curriculum

  • Introduction to Pharmacology
  • Pharmaceutical Calculations
  • Dispensing Techniques
  • Pharmacy Management
  • Practical Training & Internships

Admission Requirements

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Minimum GPA requirement
  • Application fee
  • Interview process

Duration & Fees

Duration: 1 year
Fees: USD 2,000 (subject to change)
