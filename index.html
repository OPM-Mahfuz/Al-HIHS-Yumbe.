Welcome to Al-Hassan Institute of Health Science – Yumbe

We are committed to providing excellent health education and training to meet the healthcare needs of Uganda and beyond. Our institute offers a variety of diploma and certificate programs in health sciences, equipping students with practical skills and knowledge to serve their communities.

About Us

The Al-Hassan Institute of Health Science is a leading training institution in Yumbe, Uganda, dedicated to producing qualified health professionals. Our mission is to improve community health through quality education, research, and community engagement.

Our History

Founded in 2026, our institute has rapidly grown to become a center of excellence for health education in the region. We collaborate with local health authorities and international partners to enhance our curriculum and facilities.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide accessible, accredited, and community-responsive health sciences education that equips youth with practical skills, ethical values, and professional competence, strengthening the health workforce and improving healthcare service delivery in Yumbe District and the wider West Nile region

Our Vision

A healthy and resilient society where underserved and refugee-hosting communities have equitable access to quality healthcare delivered by skilled, ethical, and locally trained health professionals

Academic Programs

Diploma in Nursing

Comprehensive training in nursing with practical placements. Duration: 3 years.

Diploma in Midwifery

Specialized training in maternal and newborn health. Duration: 2 years.

Diploma in Clinical Medicine

Training in clinical diagnosis and patient care. Duration: 3 years.

Diploma in Community Health

Focus on health promotion, disease prevention, and community engagement. Duration: 2 years.

Diploma in Pharmacy

Preparation for pharmaceutical practice and medication management. Duration: 3 years.

Diploma in Laboratory Technology

Training in diagnostic laboratory procedures. Duration: 2 years.

Certificate in Nursing

Foundation training for entry-level nurses. Duration: 1 year.

Certificate in Midwifery

Basic midwifery skills. Duration: 1 year.

Certificate in Pharmacy

Entry-level pharmaceutical training. Duration: 1 year.

Certificate in Laboratory Technology

Basic laboratory skills. Duration: 1 year.
