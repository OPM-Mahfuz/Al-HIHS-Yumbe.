Vision & Mission

Vision

A healthy and resilient society where underserved and refugee-hosting communities have equitable access to quality healthcare delivered by skilled, ethical, and locally trained health professionals.

Mission

To provide accessible, accredited, and community-responsive health sciences education that equips youth with practical skills, ethical values, and professional competence, strengthening the health workforce and improving healthcare service delivery in Yumbe District and the wider West Nile region.
